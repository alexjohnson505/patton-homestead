Town of Hamilton - Patton Property

Welcome to the Town of Hamilton's site for the Patton Estate. The Town recieved the 27 acre Patton property through the generous gift to the Town of Hamilton from Joanne Patton. The Patton Advisory Gift Committee recommended that teh Town accept the gift and the Town voted unanimously to do so at Town Meeting in April 2012. Title to the property was transferred in September 2012. This site outlines plans for the Patton Homestead, clustered Senior Housing, and the Open Space.

.

The plan for the Homestead is to create a non-profit for the management and planning of the Homestead, the Patton Archives, and the immediately surrounding grounds. In April of 2014 Town Meeting overwhelmingly approved selling some of the land for the building of 12 units of over-55 housing. Partial proceeds from the sale of land will help create an endowment for the Homestead. An application by CP Berry to develop the senior housing is currently before the Planning Board for a special permit. Real estate taxes from the 12 senior units will go toward the Town's general fund. The Town is working with Essex County Greenbelt to design a riverwalk that will be constructed in the Summer 2015, with access from the wood road along the river. The Town is also working to create parking for canoe and kayak drop off so that the river can be more readily accessed by the public from the property. Please explore this site and learn about plans for this wonderful Town asset.
