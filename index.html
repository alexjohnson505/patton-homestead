Overview

Welcome to the Town of Hamilton's site for the Patton Homestead. The 27 acre Patton property was an extremely generous gift to the Town of Hamilton from Joanne Patton. The gift was overwhelmingly accepted at Town Meeting in April 2012. The Town took title in September 2012. This site outlines plans for the Homestead, Open Space, and Senior Housing. This site also discusses the economics of the property. Please explore this site and learn about plans for this fanstastic town asset.
