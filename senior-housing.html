Senior Housing

In April 2014 Hamilton Town Meeting unanimously approved selling approximately 4-5 acres for 12 units of over-55 housing to CP Berry for ~ $1,156,000. The sales proceeds are to go to create an endowment of approximately $250,000 for the Homestead, put $156,000 into Hamilton's Affordable Housing Trust, and put the balance into the town's Stabalization Fund. The application to develop the senior housing is currently before the Planning Board for a special permit. Real estate taxes from the 12 senior units are estimated to be $148,000 per year and will go toward the Town's general fund. .
